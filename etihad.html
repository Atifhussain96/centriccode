A winning partnership

Aldar Retail has now joined hands with Etihad Guest to reward you with Etihad Guest Miles and Tier Miles, so each time you shop or dine at any one of your three preferred malls – Yas Mall, WTC Mall or Al Jimi Mall! You will earn Etihad Guest Miles that you can use for travel or redeem for future purchases across our malls.

It is true that shopping can take you places!

Know More

Buy and fly

Take home more than just your shopping:

  • Shop or dine at Yas Mall, WTC Mall and Al Jimi Mall
  • For every AED 3 you spend, we will add 1 Etihad Guest Mile and 1 Tier Mall to your account.
Know More

Why this is a big deal

  • Usually Tier Miles add up only when you fly or spend on your ADCB and FAB Etihad Guest payment cards, but now every time you shop or dine at your favourite malls, you get Tier Miles too!
  • These Tier Miles open a world of benefits when you travel, like lounge access, priority check-in and excess baggage allowance.
  • You can earn up to 20,000 Tier miles in just one year when you shop across Aldar malls. And the higher your Etihad Guest tier level, the more benefits you get. *
  • You can also link up to five UAE issued Visa cards onto the App. So, shop together and start planning your next holiday together, travelling in style.

*Etihad Guest Tiers & Benefits:

The higher your tier status, the more benefits you get:

  • Etihad Guest Silver members get lounge access, priority check in, 25% bonus Guest miles and more
  • Etihad Guest Gold members get lounge access, priority check in, priority boarding, fast track immigration, 50% bonus Guest miles, in addition to several other benefits
  • Etihad Guest Platinum members get lounge access, the chance to nominate a friend to Gold status, priority check in, priority boarding, fast track immigration, free WIFI onboard,75% bonus Guest miles, plus lots more
