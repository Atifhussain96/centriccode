Rewards by Aldar

Whether you are shopping for groceries, paying school fees, or even buying a house, Darna lets you earn and redeem rewards on just about any spend made with Aldar and our participating brands in Abu Dhabi and Al Ain.

It is the only loyalty programme in the UAE to cover such a diverse portfolio, allowing you to earn rewards in one place, and redeem them in another.
